Hello. Thank you for taking your time to check out my portfolio. My name is Sebastian. If you are looking for a Software-Engineer, Front-End Developer or a Designer you might be at the right place.

As a Software-Engineer I worked on a lot of modules or projects for the gaming industry. I created a browser based smart text editor comparable to Celtx. We needed such an editor to let characters do and say things in real time while the story creator needs no coding skills at all.

As a Front-End developer I updated the older website of an eye clinic in German-Berlin while creating the prototype and graphics for their new website or printable documents.

I also created several Designs for smaller or bigger companies. I converted high detailed vector graphics to cutter plotter friendly file sizes for multiple Tv Shows. And did various editing techniques to fulfill the customers request.

Even though I also worked as Customer Care Specialist I learned a lot about communication, speed, need and design. Helping to create a C.I. within 20-30 minutes for a company you don't know needs Skill and commitment.

In my private life I try to learn at least one new thing every single day. Sometimes more if I can. I love to checkout CSS-Tricks for new cool ideas. Btw. If you want to learn HTML/CSS or if you know someone who wants to learn but doesn't know how to start or continue after learning the simplest basics of some free app, checkout the Udemy Course The Complete 2021 Web Development Bootcamp. I don't get points or credits for sharing this link, but that course shows it's possible to teach something online that goes beyond the most basic rules.

Apart from coding I like to be outside in nature, draw, meditate or cook various dishes. I don't follow a specific cuisine. It needs to be a mix of healthy, yummy and affordable. Anyway.. what person only likes to eat just one type of cuisine? Food can be as versatile as people! If I still have time on a day I enjoy some nice gaming or reading fiction or educational books.
